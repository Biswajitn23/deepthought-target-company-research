# 1000 Company Scale-Up Plan

## Step 1: Universe Building
- DSIR R&D list
- MCA database (manufacturing NIC codes)
- Industry expos (Aero India, CPHI, Elecrama)
- BSE SME + NSE Emerge listings
- Industry association directories

Target universe: 3000–4000 companies

---

## Step 2: Filtering
Remove:
- Traders / distributors
- CROs / service companies
- PE-owned firms
- PSUs
- Companies > ₹500Cr revenue

Apply:
- E1 (Manufacturer check)
- E2 (India operational presence)

---

## Step 3: Scoring System
Apply C3–C8 scoring:
- Differentiation
- Decision-maker quality
- Sector tailwinds
- Growth signals
- Systems maturity
- Succession planning

Use AI-assisted classification + rule-based filters.

---

## Step 4: QA Layer
- Remove false positives
- Manual verification of borderline companies
- Spot-check high-confidence outputs

---

## Step 5: Output
- Final list: 1000 ICP-qualified companies
- Priority list: top 200 companies for outreach
- Fully evidence-backed dataset

---

## Timeline
Week 1: Universe building  
Week 2–3: Filtering + scoring  
Week 4: QA + final selection
