# Methodology — Target Company Research

## Objective
Identify 25 ICP-qualified Federer companies in Hyderabad across defence, aerospace, specialty chemicals, and precision engineering.

## Approach
- Selected Hyderabad due to strong defence, aerospace, and R&D ecosystem.
- Focused on manufacturing-led companies only (E1 filter).
- Removed traders, CROs, PSUs, PE-owned firms, and large corporates (>500Cr).
- Prioritized promoter-led MSMEs with technical leadership.

## Data Sources
- DSIR R&D database
- BSE SME / NSE Emerge listings
- Industry associations (ACMA, ELCINA, IDMA)
- Official company websites
- MCA filings (for leadership verification)

## Filtering Logic
- E1: Must be manufacturer
- E2: Must be India-based operational presence
- C3–C8 scoring applied to evaluate:
  - Differentiation
  - Leadership quality
  - Sector tailwinds
  - Growth signals
  - Systems maturity
  - Succession planning

## Outcome
25 companies shortlisted from ~80–100 evaluated companies.
